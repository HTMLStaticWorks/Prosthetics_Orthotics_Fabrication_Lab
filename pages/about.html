About Our Laboratory

Synthesizing advanced prosthetic socket engineering and custom orthoses since 2018. Rebuilding human confidence and performance.

Established 2018

Our Clinical Engineering Journey

ApexFlex was founded by bio-mechanical engineers who wanted to disrupt the traditional, slow plaster-cast fabrication methods. By establishing a digital scan-to-print workflow, we reduced patient socket adjustment times from weeks to just days.

Today, our laboratory serves regional orthopedic centers, providing myoelectric arm adaptations, athletic running blades, and specialized pediatric spinal braces.
