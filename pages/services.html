How We Work

Our Structural Fitting Timeline

A structured path designed to optimize fitting comfort and limb mobility metrics.

Phase 1

Biometric Scanning

Contactless laser scanners create a sub-millimeter 3D profile of the patient's residual limb.

Scan Queue: Computed in 45s
Grid Modeling: Mesh structure checks
Phase 2

CAD Socket Design

Bio-engineers design the custom socket geometry on-screen, checking for load-bearing pressure distribution.

Phase 3

Clinical Fitting & Tuning

The patient trials the finished socket. Our clinician tunes vacuum suspension levels and joint range alignments.

Fit verified: Dynamic weight balance ok
