Common Queries

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a doctor's referral to visit the lab?

Yes. As an accredited fabrication laboratory, we manufacture customized orthotic and prosthetic devices based on prescriptions provided by orthopedic surgeons, podiatrists, or physical therapists.

How long does the 3D scan and fabrication process take?

3D body sweeps require less than a minute. Standard diagnostic socket models are milled/printed in our facility within 48 to 72 hours, returning the patient for a fitting check quickly.

Does insurance cover custom prosthetic sockets?

Most major health insurance programs and Medicare cover custom-made prosthetic limbs and corrective back braces when prescribed by an accredited orthopedic physician.
