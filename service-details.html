Service Overview

Our lab designs, shapes, and builds upper and lower extremity prosthetic devices tailored directly to the patient's remaining bone, muscle volume, and desired weight load. We utilize modern carbon-fiber socket techniques, microprocessor joints, and vacuum suspension sockets to offer lightweight structures that fit perfectly without rubbing or heat retention.

Key Benefits

Enhanced Comfort

Custom socket fitting prevents tissue pressure sores and skin chafing.

Advanced Bio-Feedback

Microprocessor components provide stability and real-time step adaptation.

Patient Eligibility & Referral Guideline

This service is recommended for individuals with congenital limb difference, surgical amputations due to trauma or vascular disease, or patients seeking upgrades from mechanical joints to high-stability bionic components. Referrals require a physician prescription and clearance for physical therapy gait training.
