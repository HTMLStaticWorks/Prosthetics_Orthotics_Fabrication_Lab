Our Core Mission

Empowering Lives Through Advanced Bio-Engineering

We believe that physical limitations shouldn't hinder a rich, active life. Our facility bridges the gap between state-of-the-art medical technology and personalized rehabilitation.

Founded in 2011, our fabrication lab was established to provide higher precision alternatives to standard, mass-produced orthotic braces and heavy prosthetic limbs. By integrating advanced optical 3D scanners, proprietary CAD modeling, and multi-axis CNC carving machinery directly into our clinical workflow, we create highly-ventilated, lightweight, and anatomically perfect devices.

Facility Excellence

State-of-the-Art Technology & Materials

By eliminating manual plaster measurements, we offer a clean, highly-accurate patient experience. Our facility features advanced diagnostic and manufacturing tools:

Optical 3D Body Scanners

Captures exact contours down to 0.2mm variance. No plaster mess, and completed in less than 5 minutes.

Integrated CAD/CAM Modeling

Enables dynamic pressure simulation before physical device assembly, ensuring zero pressure hotspots.

Multi-Axis CNC Milling & 3D Printers

High-speed material milling machines and high-tensile 3D printing extruders fabricate customized diagnostic check-sockets immediately.

Clinical Standards

Our Commitment to Better Patient Outcomes

We monitor our device effectiveness through standardized outcome measurement protocols. This scientific model ensures every orthotic brace and prosthetic limb improves mobility compared to base measurements.

Gait Analysis Diagnostics

Force plates and spatial camera trackers measure hip/knee angles to correct gait imbalances.

Outcome Tracking

Structured survey assessments verify device fitment safety and comfort over 3, 6, and 12-month periods.

Physician Collaboration

We partner directly with referring specialists, sending objective gait charts, fitment measurements, and rehabilitation progression logs to clinical doctors.
